About

Builder, owner-side discipline

I build public places — schools, civic buildings, and historic landmarks — and I sweat the schedule and the budget so owners don't have to.

Thirteen years in, I've grown through nearly every seat on a Westland project: project engineer, assistant PM, project manager, superintendent, and now scheduler. That range means I read a set of drawings, a pay application, and a CPM schedule with the same fluency.

Lately my work is as much data as dirt. I built a portfolio scoring and tracking dashboard in Power BI that pulls Procore, our ERP, and SmartPM into one executive view — and I run cross-portfolio Primavera P6 scheduling for Westland's active pipeline.

I grew up in Boulder City, studied Construction & Facilities Management at BYU, and spent two years in Lima, Peru, where I earned full professional Spanish. I care about clean coordination, owner-side discipline, and finishing what I start.
